SAMPLE DATASET CREATED
==================================================

This is a synthetic dataset created for testing purposes.
To use your own dataset:

1. Replace the contents of data/raw/images/ with your satellite images
2. Replace the contents of data/raw/masks/ with corresponding oil spill masks
3. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .tif, .tiff
4. Ensure image and mask filenames match (except extension)

Dataset Sources:
- Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sudhanshu2198/oil-spill-detection
- ESA Copernicus: https://scihub.copernicus.eu/
- NASA Earth Data: https://earthdata.nasa.gov/
